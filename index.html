My Hobby

My favourite hobby is chess, because it is a fun way to keep your mind sharp, and you don't need to be good at anything in particular to play with someone.
Chess originated in India before spreading to Persia where the term 'checkmate' was coined from the Persian phrase 'shah mat' (the king is dead)
Here are some fun facts about chess!

  • Chess is over 1,500 years old!
  • There are more possible chess games than atoms in the universe!
  • The longest game of chess lasted 20 hours!

I will now tell you how each of the pieces move.
The pawn only moves forward, but captures diagonally.
The knight moves 2 squares straight, and 1 square to either the left or right, making an 'L' shape. The knight also has the ability to jump over pieces, hence it's shape, a horse.
The bishop moves in diagonals, diagonally moving on squares the color of the square it started on.
The rook moves forward, backward, left and right on any color square.
The queen is the most valuable piece because it can move like a bishop and a rook, but if a queen started on a black square, by using its rook movement, it can also go along white square diagonals by moving to a white square and then doing bishop movement.
The king can move just like the queen, but only 1 square. If the king is attacked in a way that cannot be defended, it is known as checkmate
